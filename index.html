About me

I have just left high school and am hopefully going to do an HND in Visual Communication in User Experience at Edinburgh College after the summer.

After a difficult high school experience, I have left with a Higher in Design Manufacture and Applications of Maths. These subjects have given me great problem-solving skills.

For the last two years, I have attended a Foundation Apprenticeship in Digital Media and Design at Edinburgh College. This course has enabled me to expand my ideas and designs. I have been able to experiment and try new media, such as typography and illustration. It has been useful to work on briefs and do the research, whether in a group or by myself. Unfortunately, due to different factors, the college was unable to provide us with an apprenticeship.
