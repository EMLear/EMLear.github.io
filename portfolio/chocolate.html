Chocolate bar packaging

Brief

Client: Chocolatarium

The brief was to work in a group to design three chocolate bar wrappers. The wrappers had to have a theme of Scottish mythical creatures. The three creatures chosen were Unicorn, Selkie, and Faerie. I worked on the Unicorn.

As a group in this project, we had to research Scottish mythology, create colour palettes and mood boards, research the target market, high-end and low-end competitors, the average pricing per chocolate content, the history of chocolate, and how chocolate is made.

During the design process, I found that my initial design didn't match the rest of my group's designs. This was because I had included a photograph in my design, while the group had used only illustration. Upon reflection, I decided that I could not make the design work, so I changed it to an illustrated approach.
