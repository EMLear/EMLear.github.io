Fringe campaign

Brief

/Client: Edinburgh Fringe Festival

Part 1

You are the designer, and your aim is to produce a poster, programme and visual merchandise for the Edinburgh Festival Fringe website to promote The Edinburgh Fringe Festival 2024. All poster designs must include: Edinburgh Festival Fringe Logo, Festival Dates, Festival Website and eye catching imagery which can be applied to the poster, tote bag and sweatshirt merchandise.

Part 2

The brief is to create 18 Instagram posts that follow on from part 1.These posts must include : 3 acts and 5 sponsors and it must work with the poster you created for the Fringe. These Instagram posts must be attention grabbing and be for all ages.
