Fringe campaign

Brief

Client: Edinburgh Fringe Festival

Part 1

You are the designer, and your aim is to produce a poster, programme, and visual merchandise for the Edinburgh Festival Fringe website to promote The Edinburgh Fringe Festival 2024. All poster designs must include: the Edinburgh Festival Fringe logo, festival dates, festival website, and eye-catching imagery which can be applied to the poster, tote bag, and sweatshirt merchandise.

For Part 1 of the project, I had to research colour schemes and mood boards, tourism in Edinburgh, Edinburgh Fringe merchandise, analyse past Fringe posters, and research artists for inspiration.

Part 2

The brief is to create 18 Instagram posts that follow on from Part 1. These posts must include: 3 acts and 5 sponsors, and they must work with the poster you created for the Fringe. These Instagram posts must be attention-grabbing and suitable for all ages.

For Part 2 of the project, I looked at other festivals' Instagram campaigns for inspiration, made a planning board for the campaign, and chose three performers and five sponsors.
