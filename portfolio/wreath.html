Christmas caroling poster

Brief

Client: Granton College

A group of second-year students in the foundational course—seven in total—were tasked with creating a poster for the Granton Christmas Singalong. The poster had to be non-religious and inclusive. It also had to include the information provided.

We were given two weeks to create the poster, which was then judged by a panel, and the favourite was displayed around the Granton area.

This project was a real time crunch and was designed to give us a taste of working in the graphics world with tight deadlines.
